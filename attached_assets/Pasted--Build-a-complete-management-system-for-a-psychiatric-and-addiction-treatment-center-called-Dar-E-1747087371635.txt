
 `Build a complete management system for a psychiatric and addiction treatment center called "Dar El Hayah". The system should manage patients, staff, financials, and therapeutic groups with the following features:  1. **Patient Management:**    - Add new patients with a custom daily rate.    - Track admission date and calculate total stay cost.    - Add financial items per patient such as:      - Cigarettes      - Medical tests      - Medications      - Personal expenses      - Haircut      - Buffet      - Laundry    - Discharge or delete patients.    - Generate patient account statements:      - Payments made (with dates)      - Monthly total cost      - Amount paid vs. remaining balance  2. **Payment Collection System:**    - Select patient, add payment amount, and assign collection staff and date.    - Automatically credit the payment to the patient’s balance.  3. **Staff Management:**    - Register new staff with roles:      - General Manager      - Shift Manager (Halfway or Detox)      - Supervisor (Halfway or Detox)      - Worker      - Chef      - Doctor      - Therapist      - Case Referral Officer    - Manage salaries.    - Handle loans, deductions, bonuses, and points (bounuses).  4. **Therapeutic Group System:**    - Create and manage therapy groups.    - Assign patients and supervising therapists.    - Each patient has a treatment stage that can be edited daily.    - Therapists and supervisors can only edit reports of the same day.    - Weekly evaluations for each group.  5. **Daily Expense Tracking:**    - Add daily expenses under categories like:      - Kitchen      - Buffet      - Rent      - Maintenance      - Medications      - Electricity      - Gas      - Water      - Transportation      - Bonuses      - Salaries (added automatically to staff accounts)      - Miscellaneous      - Hospitality      - Recreational day  6. **Reports:**    - Daily report: income, expenses, balance.    - Monthly report: income, expenses, balance.    - Mid-year and annual reports.    - Profit and loss statement (monthly).    - Daily report for patients with negative balances.  7. **User Roles and Permissions:**    - Add new users with roles.    - Therapist role can:      - Access assigned therapy groups.      - Write/edit daily and weekly patient reports for current day only.      - Cannot add/remove patients from groups.    - Full permissions only granted to system administrator.  Build the system using Python (Flask or Django preferred), with a web-based frontend using HTML/CSS/JS. Use a database (SQLite or PostgreSQL) to store all data. Ensure secure login, session management, and clean, modern UI. `
